Cleaner Bot

A game by Martin Vilcans

Made in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 26

I had the perfect apartment. Light and airy, it reflected my personality. No fluff, just 220 square meters of clean and simple Scandinavian design.

But I also had a Jack Russell terrier, all white. I always make sure he only eats ecological low-carb food, but I must have made a mistake, and now there's puke all over the apartment. There's no way I can spend a second there.

I'm lucky to have a remote controlled cleaning bot and surveillance cameras. I can control the bot with the WASD keys. The cameras switch automatically by motion detection.
